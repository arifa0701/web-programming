Sweet Home

"Sweet Home" is a South Korean Netflix series based on the popular webtoon of the same name. The story follows Cha Hyun-soo, a reclusive high school student who moves into a new apartment after losing his family. Suddenly, humans begin turning into monsters, and Hyun-soo must fight to survive alongside other residents of the building.

Key Information

  • Release Date: December 18, 2020
  • Genre: Horror, Thriller, Drama
  • Seasons: 1
  • Episodes: 10
  • Based on: Webtoon by Kim Carnby and Hwang Young-chan

Main Characters

  • Cha Hyun-soo: A loner who becomes a key figure in the fight against the monsters.
  • Seo Yi-kyung: A former firefighter and skilled fighter.
  • Pyeon Sang-wook: A mysterious man with a dark past.
  • Lee Eun-hyuk: A resourceful and intelligent resident.
