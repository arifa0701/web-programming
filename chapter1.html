Pasta Recipe

Pasta is a classic Italian recipe,Its easy and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 400gm Pasta
  • 6 cloves garlic,thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp red chilli flakes
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large pan.
  3. Add the red chili flakes to the pan and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat it.
  5. Season with salt to taste and garnish with chopped parsley.
